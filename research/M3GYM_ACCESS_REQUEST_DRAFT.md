## Texto em inglês

We request access to the M3GYM dataset exclusively for non-commercial academic research. Our objective is to externally validate a 2D computer-vision and biomechanical analysis engine across Pilates movement protocols. We intend to evaluate pose landmark coverage, repetition counting, projected joint-angle agreement, camera-view suitability, occlusion robustness, and confidence calibration.

M3GYM videos, images, annotations, meshes, and derived subject-level data will remain isolated from all commercial systems and repositories. They will not be redistributed, incorporated into the PilatesVision product, or used to train commercial model weights. Only aggregate results permitted by the final data-access agreement will be reported. Access will be limited to approved researchers and stored in a restricted research environment.
